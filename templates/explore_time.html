Game rules: Two players take turns placing black or white pieces on the board. The first player to get 4 pieces on a row, column or diagonal, wins the game! When the board is full, the game is a draw

Experimental procedure: We recruited 30 participants to played against AI opponents for 60 minutes, with a time limit of 5, 10 or 20 seconds per move. The time limit was constant within each game, and changed between games. Exceeding the time limit counted as a game loss. After each game, the AI changed its settings. When the participant won, the AI became stronger, when the participant lost, the AI became weaker. We compensated participants $12 but gave no performance bonus.
