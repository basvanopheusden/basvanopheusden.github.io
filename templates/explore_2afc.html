Game rules: Two players take turns placing black or white pieces on the board. The first player to get 4 pieces on a row, column or diagonal, wins the game! When the board is full, the game is a draw

Experimental procedure: We recruited 30 participants. Each participant played against AI opponents for 30 minutes and completed 82 trials each of a two-alternative forced-choice task and an evaluation task. On a 2AFC trial, the participant sees a board position and two candidate moves (dashed circles), and indicates (cross) which move he or she would prefer. We compensated participants $12 for their time, but gave no performance bonus.
